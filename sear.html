Sear Fusion Home Dining

The Home KBBQ Experience

Role: UX Designer

Timeline: 2 Weeks (June 2022)

Tools: Figma

Challenge

Sear is a Korean barbeque service where the experience of eating Korean barbeque can be enjoyed at the comfort of the customers' home. Originating in 2021 during a period of lockdown, Sear delivers high-quality restaurant dishes coupled with a distinctive home-dining atmosphere. Seeking to enhance their online presence, Sear sought help in revamping their website to enhance conversions and ensure a smooth digital customer journey.

Upon understanding the client's objectives, we pinpointed the challenges within the customer journey. Subsequently, we devised a tailored solution to address these pain points and align with the client's goals.

Original Website

Approach

We conducted a website analysis of Sear's website and some of the findings we found include:

  • A complex sitemap that resulted in an unclear user journey.
  • A cognitive overload of information that would not allow users to not navigate properly.
  • Atmosphere of website resembled an ecommerce website that showcased products over offering a service.
  • Backend of website could be adjusted to reduce unnecessary costs.

Solution

To address the issues and achieve Sear's goals, we redesigned the entire website to provide a better user experience. The new changes included:

  • We created new graphics to showcase the home KBBQ experience and the great foods offered.
  • Removed any unnecessary sections and pages that would cause users to get lost navigating.
  • Implemented a consistent brand guide to make the website have better readability and usability.
  • Uninstalled plugins that could be substitued with alternatives to automate tasks.
  • Implemented a Build Your Own Banquet custom function for users to create their own experience.

Outcome

The redesign of the Sear website received positive feedback from the client and their customers. The updated graphics enriched the website's theme and drew in more visitors, leading to a 48% surge in traffic. The newly introduced custom Build Your Own Banquet feature contributed to a seamless checkout process, resulting in a 64% boost in conversion rate.
