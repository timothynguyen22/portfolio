VR Postcard

Postcard from the Future

Role: 3D Modeller, Programmer

Timeline: 2 Weeks (March 2020)

Tools: Blender, A-frame

The VR Postcard was created for Designing for Virtual Reality at the Univeristy of Sydney. It aimed to provide an insight of what a VR postcard would look like in 20 years. The purpose of the application was to raise awareness around climate change and its effects.
