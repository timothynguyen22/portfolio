ABOUT

Hi again, my name is Timothy. I design and create things (most of the time). I’m currently the Head of Web Design & Web Development at Cinemano. I consult with clients about their web design needs and try to improve the user experience of the website.

I graduated from the University of Sydney with a Bachelor of Design Computing. I fell in love with user experience as I really enjoyed finding new solutions to improve the lives of others, primarily through design. It opened my eyes to a whole different aspect of design and innovation.

As you may have seen in the hero section, I really do believe in always finding new ways to improve myself and everything around me. I don't think I'm perfect but I do think I can continuously learn! That's why I thorougly enjoy reading self development books as I guess they're like 'life hacks' to me. It gives me insight on how to better myself mentally, emotionally and physically. I also enjoy trying new sports, even though my hand eye coordination needs some work but I guess that's why I try it 😅
