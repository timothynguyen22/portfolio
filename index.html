Case Study - Design Innovation

Kilowatt is a mobile application that encourages your community to reduce household energy usage through rewards and incentives. It provides live household energy tracking and incentives your community to meet energy saving goals. By working towards community energy goals, users can earn points that can be redeemed for energy utilities discounts.

Case Study - User Interaction

Self Help Virtual Pet (Vpet) is a mobile application that assists and aims to encourage users to maintain a healthy lifestyle by providing a personal virtual pet. The virtual pet is illustrated as a reflection of the user and to ensure the user adheres to their healthy lifestyle goals, the pet will be healthy and survive as the user meets these goals.

Case Study - Interactive Product Design Studio

The CURE (Community Under ReEducation) Project was created to educate players on the symptoms of COVID-19, how they should respond if they suspect themselves or others of infection. It is a virtual reality contruct created with Unity. The concept was created due to the rapid growth of the Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) that has attributed to the misinformation and misunderstanding of safety practices.
