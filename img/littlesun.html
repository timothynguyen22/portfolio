Little Sun Mockup Campaign

The Little Sun and Solar Citizens mockup capaign was created for the Visual Communications class at the Univeristy of Sydney. It aimed to illustrate visual communications techniques to showcase a mockup campaign about non-profit charities. The purpose of the mockup of the campaign was to highlight and market the charities' goal.
