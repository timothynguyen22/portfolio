Hello there again,

I'm Timothy Nguyen, an aspiring UX and UI designer from Sydney. I graduated from the University of Sydney with a Bachelor of Design Computing. I fell in love with user experience as I grew fond of finding new solutions to improve the lives of others, primarily through design. I want to always aim to design innovative products and create better experiences for others.

I chose to study Design Computing because technology has always played a large role in my life and being able to see it constantly innovated and developed, sparked an interest in wanting to create new products. Studying Design Computing allowed me to gain insight in understanding problems and reframing it to uncover potential solutions. Design Computing provided different perspectives for situations and taught me to always keep trying to find new but suitable answers.
